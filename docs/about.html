About Me

My interests include sports, videogames, music and anime.

As a New Jersey kid, New York has had a big influence on my life. I'm a big fan of all New York sport teams, not including hockey or soccer. My favorite teams are the New York Yankees, New York Giants and New York Knicks. These are some of the biggest franchises in the world and have been apart of me for as long as I can remember. It has been hard the past decade, but I am confident things will turn around.

I have played videogames for the longest time. My earliest memory dates back to the Wii, more specifically Wii Sports and Just Dance. I loved these games growing up and continue to enjoy both of these timeless greats. As I have gotten older, my taste has shifted towards first person shooting games. The top of my favorites include Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch, Valorant and Fortnite. But I have continued to play sports games. I've refraiend from buying NBA 2K and Madden every year as they have decreased in quality over the past couple of years.

My favorite music includes hip-hop, raeggaton, and bachata. My parents always played spanish music in the house which has rubbed off on me. Some of my favorite are A Boogie with a Hoodie, Lil Uzi Vert, and 21 Savage. They each have their own style and vibe to their songs which is what makes me love listening to their music.

When I was younger I was never into anime. I thought it was weird because of the Naruto kids who ran down the hallways with their arms flailling behind them. A coworker introduced me back in 2020, during COVID, so i gave it a shot. I was suggested to watch Attack on Titan which is still my favorite to this day. Since then, I have watched several anime shows such as Jujitsu Kaisen, Tokyo Ghoul and Jojo's Bizarre Adventure.
